Frequently asked questions

Use the finder to narrow down ideas quickly, or browse the full list of categories on the homepage. If something is missing, send us a note and we will add it.

How does the gift finder work?

Choose a category, set a budget, and pick a style and sentiment level. The tool scores ideas based on those preferences and shows the best matches first.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Gift-Kind works without signups and keeps your answers in your browser.

Are any of the links affiliate links?

Some links are affiliate links. If you buy through them, Gift-Kind may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Can I filter for practical vs meaningful gifts?

Yes. Use the Gift Style and Sentiment controls to get practical upgrades, thoughtful ideas, or keepsakes.

Is Gift-Kind suitable for UK shoppers?

Yes. The recommendations and links are UK-friendly and focus on items that are easy to buy locally.

What categories do you cover?

We cover people, occasions, milestones, baby and parent gifts, teacher gifts, and difficult-to-buy-for categories. Browse them all on the homepage.

Can I use the finder for last-minute gifts?

Absolutely. Choose practical options and smaller budgets to get quick, easy-to-buy ideas.

Where can I find Mother’s Day or Baby Shower ideas?

Start with the relevant category page: Mother’s Day or Baby Shower.

Can I suggest new categories?

Yes. Send a quick note via the contact form and we’ll prioritise the most requested categories.
