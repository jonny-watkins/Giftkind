Privacy Policy

Short, clear, and designed for a simple static site.

Summary

GiftKind is a static site. Your answers stay in your browser and are not sent to our servers.

We do not require accounts or logins. We do not ask for personal data to use the gift finder.

Data we don't collect

  • Your gift answers or preferences.
  • Login credentials (we do not offer logins).
  • Payment details (payments happen on Stripe if you choose to buy premium).

Payments and affiliate links

If you choose to unlock premium features, you are redirected to Stripe Checkout. Stripe processes payments directly.

GiftKind may include affiliate links to retailers. Clicking a link will take you to that retailer's site, which has its own privacy policy.

Analytics

We use basic analytics to understand site usage (page views and button clicks). This may set cookies in your browser. You can block cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Contact

Questions? Use the contact form on the homepage and we will reply by email.
